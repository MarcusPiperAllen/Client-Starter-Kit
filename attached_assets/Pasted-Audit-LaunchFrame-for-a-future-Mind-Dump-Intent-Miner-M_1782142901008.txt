Audit LaunchFrame for a future “Mind Dump → Intent Miner → Master Build Prompt” workflow.

Do not change code.

Definition:
A “mind dump” means the user records or writes a messy long-form explanation of what they want to build. It may be repetitive, unclear, out of order, emotional, or incomplete.

Example:
“I want to build an app for small business owners… maybe coaches too… it should help them organize their website ideas… I don’t want them filling out a million fields… maybe they paste a transcript… the app should figure out the business type, goals, audience, services, pages, SEO, and then create one strong prompt for Claude Code or Replit Agent…”

The future product should mine that messy input and extract structured intent.

The system should identify:

* Project/app name ideas
* Business or organization name
* Target audience
* Primary goal
* Business type
* Services/features
* Pages/screens needed
* Tone/style
* Technology needs
* SEO needs
* Missing questions
* One master build prompt

Tasks:

1. Inspect the current LaunchFrame app.
2. List every current intake field.
3. For each field, classify:

   * Must keep as manual input
   * Could be inferred from a mind dump
   * Should become optional
   * Might be redundant
4. Review current generated outputs.
5. Identify which outputs should remain separate.
6. Identify which outputs should be merged into one “Master Build Prompt.”
7. Recommend how a future “Mind Dump” input section should work.
8. Recommend what the app should do after the user pastes a transcript.
9. Identify what data structure/schema the extracted intent should produce.
10. Recommend the next 5 product improvements.
